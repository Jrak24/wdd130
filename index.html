Madagascar

Madagascar is an island country comprising the island of Madagascar and numerous smaller peripheral islands. Lying off the southeastern coast of Africa, it is the world's fourth largest island, the second-largest island country, and the 46th largest country in the world. Its capital and largest city is Antananarivo.Having developed in isolation, the island nation is famed for its unique wildlife. Traditionally, the economy has been based on the cultivation of paddy rice, coffee, vanilla and cloves.
