Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about rafting adventures, safety tips, and booking options for enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Audience

The target audience includes adventure seekers looking for thrilling experiences, families interested in safe outdoor activities, and travel enthusiasts exploring new destinations.

Scenario Questions:

  • What types of rafting adventures are available?
  • Is there any age restriction for participants?
  • How can I book a rafting trip?

Answers:

  • Various levels of rafting adventures are offered, from calm waters for beginners to challenging rapids for experienced rafters.
  • Participants must be at least 8 years old, and there are specific requirements for younger participants based on trip difficulty.
  • Booking can be done online through the website or by contacting our customer service.
