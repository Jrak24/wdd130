Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about whitewater rafting services offered, including trip details, safety information, and booking options. It aims to attract adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and families looking for an exciting outdoor activity.

Audience

Our target audience includes:

  • Adventure enthusiasts looking for thrilling experiences.
  • Families interested in outdoor activities.
  • Groups planning team-building events or outings.
Scenario Questions:
  1. Who are the adventure enthusiasts seeking thrilling experiences?
    Answer: Individuals aged 18-45 who enjoy outdoor adventures and extreme sports.
  2. What kind of families are interested in outdoor activities?
    Answer: Families with children aged 8 and above who enjoy spending time in nature and participating in water activities.
  3. What groups are planning team-building events or outings?
    Answer: Corporate groups and organizations looking for team-building exercises and recreational activities.
