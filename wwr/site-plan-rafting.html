Purpose

The purpose of our rafting site is to provide information about our rafting trips and company history, and to encourage adventure seekers to join our expeditions.

Audience

Target Audience

Our target audience includes adventure seekers, families looking for a fun outdoor activity, and corporate teams seeking team-building experiences.

Scenarios

  • A family looking for a weekend adventure.
  • A corporate team building event organizer.
  • An individual looking for an extreme sports experience.

Site Layout

The site layout will include a homepage, about page, trips page, and contact page.
